The Coffee Conundrum & Our Opportunity

The Problem We Solve

  • ●Expensive Premium Coffee: Major chains offer great coffee but at prices inaccessible for daily consumption by many.
  • ●Inconsistent Low-End Options: Basic street vendors often lack quality, consistency, and a premium experience.
  • ●Convenience Gap: A clear lack of quick, high-quality coffee options in high-footfall areas like commute routes and office parks, especially in bustling cities like Mumbai.

The Market Is Brewing

India's coffee culture is experiencing explosive growth. Reports from 2024-2025 (e.g., UnivDatos, Grand View Research) indicate the India Coffee Retail Chains market was valued at ~USD 615 million in 2024, projected to grow at a CAGR of around 8.2%. The Specialty Coffee market in India is even more dynamic, expected to reach ~USD 6.28 billion by 2030 with a CAGR of 13.6% (2025-2030).

Key drivers include rising disposable incomes, urbanization, and a growing café culture, particularly among millennials and Gen Z in cities like Mumbai (TechSci Research, 2024). There's a clear shift towards premium and specialty coffee experiences, with consumers valuing quality, origin, and unique flavors (Godrej Food Trends Report 2024, Restaurant India 2025).

Sources: Based on data from UnivDatos, Grand View Research, TechSci Research (2024-2025 reports).

Our Business Blueprint

Our focus is on quality, speed, and a memorable experience. We serve a curated menu of classic espresso drinks, ensuring every cup is perfect.

Core Menu

  • Espresso & Americano
  • Cappuccino & Latte
  • Mocha & Flavored Lattes
  • Iced Coffees & Cold Brew

Our Service Promise

  • Fast & Efficient: Optimized for quick takeaway service.
  • Friendly Interaction: Professional and welcoming baristas.
  • Unique Ambiance: The cart itself is a key part of the premium experience.
  • Quality Ingredients: Freshly roasted beans, quality milk.

Our lean and scalable business model is designed to maximize profitability by minimizing fixed overheads, a key advantage of our mobile approach.

Revenue & Pricing

Revenue comes from direct sales of beverages. Our pricing strategy offers high value, positioning us significantly below premium cafes (e.g., ₹115 for a standard 8oz milk coffee) but above basic street vendors.

Cost Structure

Our main costs are variable (COGS like beans, milk, cups) and operational (staff, power, transport, maintenance). By avoiding high fixed rent, we maintain a flexible and efficient cost base.

Why Mobile over a Fixed Kiosk?

We chose the mobile cart model for its strategic advantages in **location flexibility**, allowing us to test markets and go where demand is highest, and its **lower fixed overheads**, which enable our affordable pricing. This agility and cost-efficiency are central to our strategy to make premium coffee accessible.

Our unique blend of quality, price, experience, and mobility creates a strong competitive advantage that is difficult to replicate.

vs. Premium Cafes: We offer comparable quality at a significantly lower price point with greater convenience for takeaway customers.

vs. Basic Vendors: We provide vastly superior quality, consistency, professional branding, and a better overall experience.

Our Core USP: The intersection of these four pillars—Premium Quality, Affordable Price, Unique Aesthetic, and Strategic Mobility—is our key differentiator.
